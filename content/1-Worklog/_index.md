**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Account Setup and Basic Networking (Modules 1 & 2)](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Advanced Storage and Compute Introduction (Modules 3 & 4)](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Advanced Compute and Databases (Module 4 & Databases)](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Expanded Databases and Monitoring (Databases & Monitoring)](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Server and Application Migration (Module 2)](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Database Migration and Hybrid Networking (Module 2)](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Cost Optimization and Performance (Module 3)](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Reliability and Advanced Security](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Microservices and Serverless](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Container Orchestration](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Data Lake and Analytics](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [AI/ML Foundations](1.12-week12/)
